Mutton Curry

Description

The Indian Mutton Curry is an essential part of the Indian diet. It is spicy, it is sexy and it is not recommended for the faint hearted.

Ingrediants

  • Mutton
  • Masala
  • Paprika
  • Tomatoe

Instructions

  1. Clean the Mutton
  2. Sautte the onions
  3. Prepare the Masala
  4. Let it cook like its hot
